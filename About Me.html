About Me

Hello! My name is Patrick Day! I graduated from James Madison University with a B.S in Geographic Science. My concentration was in Applied Geographic Information Systems. I received the USGIF certificate in Geospatial Intelligence.

I have experience with Esri products such as ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online. I also am well versed in computer programming. I have used Python scripting and automation for GIS projects, and I have also done some front end web development using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I am eager to learn and continue to expand my knowledge of GIS and programming daily.
